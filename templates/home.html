Your farm in your home

Wax coatings, hormones injections & chemical sprays. Yet there is no other source for our vegetables, so we end up buying from groceries. Even if you buy organic, is there a way to check its authenticity for yourself?

On top of the health effects caused by consuming these veggies, we are also impacting the environment in a negative way. There is a huge amount of CO2 emitted in order to process and transport these vegetables to your home.

Karat Farms wants to address this situation. We believe there is a solution which gives the people more power, transparency and freedom.

Karat farms jumps on the opportunities provided by technology to create an innovative and sustainable solution so you can grow your vegetables at ease from your home!

Mission

To provide accessible, affordable & simple solutions to empower people to grow their own food

Vision

To be a trusted resource for urban farmers all over India

Product features

Organic, Non-GMO seeds and saplings

Soilless potting mix that boosts plant growth and eliminates soil borne pests.

Plant based growbag material that keeps just the right amount of moisture and lets the roots breathe

Raised height to avoid waterlogging on your terrace and keep the roof safe

Automated, smart irrigation that waters the plants at fixed times and can be controlled remotely

Your terrace is your farm

We are all well aware of the unethical practices used to sell vegetables. Wax coatings, hormones injections & chemical sprays. Yet there is no other source for our vegetables, so we end up buying from them. Even if you buy organic. Is there a way to check its authenticity for yourself? Not really! and this is what scares us.

On top of the health effects caused by consuming these vegetables, we are also impacting the environment in a negative way. There is a huge amount of CO2 emitted in order to process and transport these vegetables to your home.

Karat Farms wants to address this situation. We believe there is a solution which gives the people more power, transparency and freedom.

Karat farms jumps on the opportunities provided by technology to create an innovative and sustainable solution so you can grow your vegetables at ease from your home!
